Inspiration

koffiePot recognizes that the MCB PAPS system lacks a certain amount of scoring flexibility as well as clarity in order to function well. koffiePot therefore has been designed to shift the rigid, unflexible, and lack of user friendlyness away from the PDF document. koffiePot does all the annoying calculations, so you don't have to. All the department has to do is agree on its own way of calculating "A" to "E" grades. The grade will then be brewed by the koffiePot app and the result will tell you exactly what digits to put into the PDF to get the desired result. You're welcome! ;)
